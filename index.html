About the Author:
Michelle Zauner

Michelle Zauner is an American musician, singer, songwriter, and author. She is the lead vocalist of the band Japanese Breakfast, and the author of a best-selling book: Crying in H Mart.

She was raised in Eugene, Oregon, and she describes her childhood with her family in Oregon in her memoir. The memoir, published in 2021, explores her Asian-American identity in relation to her struggles with grief, and has since gained many awards and praise.

















H Mart and Sunrise Market

Michelle often went to H Mart and Sunrise Market with her mother to go grocery shopping together. She describes shopping with her mom as one of the times they could bond together, and it made Michelle feel connected to her Korean culture. After her mother passed away, she had to learn how to navigate these markets, that were so familiar to her mother, by herself.
